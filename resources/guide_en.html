Inspired from the Noob universe of OLYDRI studio, Horizon Alpha let you live the complexity of leading the incomparable Noob guild. Use the strengths and weaknesses of Arthéon, Omega Zell, Gaea and Sparadrap in order to reach the level 100.

Only 2 objectives :

  • Kill all fiends : 'The Bad People'.
  • Keep alive all members of the Noob guild : Arthéon, Omega Zell, Gaea and Sparadrap.

Arthéon, the Warrior

Arthéon is the leader of the Noob guild. Each day, he needs to cope with the members of his guild and litteraly take hits in their stead.

In Horizon Alpha, he can attrack fiends towards him. In this case, he becomes their first target to hit. By staying put, this skills duration increase. The received hits lower the duration and then his life. Moving reduce the duration to 0. In that case, fiends are now free to attack any member of the guild.

Omega Zell, the Assassin

Omega Zell is above all else arrogant, narcissist, misogynist and despises the weaks.

In Horizon Alpha, his attack skill is strongly related to the attacks of the other members of the guild. The more damage they do, the more Omega Zell will be able to. He can also skip his turn in order to improve his attack skill.

Gaea, the Elemental Summoner

Gaea is penny-pinching, make all necessary effort in order to avoid paying and always aim to gain even more money.

In Horizon Alpha, she can charge her spell, turn by turn, and then cast it to a fiend. However, when a fiend dies, a loot appears at the same location. As long as the loot is present on the battle field, Gaea would not be able to concentrate. She can however move instantly to catch it. When moving or when receiving damage, her concentration is also broken and all accumulated charge is lost.

Sparadrap, the Priest

Sparadrap is est clumsy, the biggest noob ever, or a genius player hidding his true skills. Opinions differ.

In Horizon Alpha, his main skill is healing. But, in order to charge his skill, he needs first to heal a fiend! If his 'skill' is fully loaded, the heal skill becomes an instant death skill. Any target will die, friend or foe.
